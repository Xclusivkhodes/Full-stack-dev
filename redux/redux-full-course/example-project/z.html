PAAIS

Hack-AI-Thon

Stop waiting for the future. Build It.

Build AI solutions rooted in local data, culture, and impact across five key sectors: HealthTech, AgriTech, FinTech, ClimateTech, and EdTech. It is time to scale Africa's ethical AI ecosystem.

Step up and build relevant local AI solutions using Afrocentric data. Compete, connect and prove your skills, showcase your AI-led innovations in live demos, and get hands-on guidance in our exclusive mentorship labs for your start-up and investment to scale up.

Ready to forge cross-sector partnerships and drive sustainable development? Bring your boldest ideas to the Pan African AI & Innovation Summit. Compete, connect, and showcase your youth-led AI innovations to global tech leaders and investors.

Event Structure

  • Pre-Hack Bootcamp: Virtual training/sync on rules of engagement (ROE), datasets, and design
  • 48-Hour Hack Sprint: Hybrid format (Accra + virtual teams)
  • Demo Day: Finalists pitch live at the Summit

Innovation Tracks

  • HealthTech: AI for healthcare, diagnostics, treatment and wellness
  • AgriTech: Crop yield, pest and disease detection, supply chain
  • FinTech: Tools for financial inclusion, payment, fraud detection & prevention
  • ClimateTech: Flood alerts, waste management, energy
  • EdTech: Language-inclusive platforms, AI tutors, AI platforms for learning

Eligibility & Rewards

  • Who: African developers, researchers, students, and startups
  • Teams: 3–5 members with cross-disciplinary skills
  • Requirements: Must use AI with local relevance
  • Prizes: Cash prizes, mentorship, and exhibition slots
  • Perks: Access to partner accelerators

Key Dates

  • Apply: 15th - 15th July
  • Bootcamp: Aug 20–22
  • Hackathon: Sept 6–8
  • Demo Day: Sept 22 - 23 @ PAAiS
